Real Automation Success Stories

1

Product Data Migration Automation

Challenge: A mid-sized retailer needed to migrate thousands of product listings from an old system to a new e-commerce platform, ensuring accuracy and category compliance.

Solution: Created a set of automated scripts to clean, transform, and import product data while validating fields against business rules.

Result: Reduced migration time from weeks to just two days, eliminated manual errors, and ensured all products met the new platform's standards.

2

Jira Process Automation

Challenge: A large OEM faced inefficiencies managing complex Jira workflows, with teams spending hours each week on repetitive ticket updates, status changes, and reporting.

Solution: Developed a suite of Python scripts to automate Jira tasks, including ticket creation, status transitions, custom field updates, and automated reporting.

Result: Saved 8 hours weekly per team, reduced manual errors, and ensured faster, more consistent project tracking.

3

Graphic Package Production Automation

Challenge: A startup needed to produce hundreds of graphic packages, each with precise formatting and packaging parameters, which was time-consuming and error-prone when done manually.

Solution: Built custom automations to generate, package, and update these graphic assets according to exact specifications.

Result: Significantly reduced production time, streamlined updates, and ensured consistent quality across all packages.

4

International Sales Accounting Automation

Challenge: A small company faced challenges managing accounting for international sales, dealing with multiple currencies, tax rules, and manual data entry.

Solution: Implemented automated scripts to import sales data, convert currencies, apply correct tax rates, and generate accounting reports.

Result: Reduced accounting workload by several hours weekly, minimized errors, and ensured compliance with international tax regulations.
